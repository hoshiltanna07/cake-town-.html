Our Story

Baked with heart
in Kansas

Cake Town was born from a simple belief: every celebration deserves something extraordinary. Located in the heart of Garden City, Kansas, we handcraft cakes that taste as beautiful as they look.

From the creamiest baked cheesecakes to towering wedding centerpieces, our team pours genuine love and artistry into each creation. We work closely with every customer to make your cake more than dessert — it's a memory.

"Baking has always been my passion, and with Cake Town, I've turned that passion into my profession."
